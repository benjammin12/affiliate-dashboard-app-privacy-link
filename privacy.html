Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Mom Group Dashboard ("the App") is a private tool used to manage Facebook Page and Group content, generate posts, and view audience analytics. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

1. Who This App Is For

This is a private, single-user application. It is not distributed publicly and is used solely by the administrator of the associated Facebook Page and Group.

2. Data We Access

  • Facebook OAuth token — used to authenticate with the Facebook Graph API on your behalf. Stored temporarily in a server-side session and never written to disk or a database.
  • Facebook Page information — page name, follower count, post engagement data, and audience insights. Fetched in real time and displayed in the dashboard only.
  • Facebook Page posts and comments — read to display analytics and recent activity. Not stored beyond your session.
  • Uploaded analytics files (.xlsx) — processed in memory to display insights. Not saved to disk after processing.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store personal data from Facebook Group members.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking beyond the session required for Facebook login.
  • We do not retain Facebook data after your session ends.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Facebook Graph API — used to read page and post data. Governed by Meta's Data Policy.
  • OpenAI / Anthropic APIs — used to generate post content. Only the topic and post type you provide are sent; no personal or audience data is included in these requests.
  • Amazon Product Advertising API — used to search for products. No personal data is sent.

5. Data Retention

No data is stored persistently. All Facebook tokens and fetched data exist only for the duration of your browser session and are cleared when the session ends or you disconnect your account.

6. Your Rights

You can disconnect your Facebook account at any time using the "Disconnect" button in the app, which clears your session token immediately. You may also revoke app access directly from your Facebook App Settings.

7. Contact

This app is privately operated. For any questions, contact the app administrator directly.
